What is Tau Beta Sigma?

"Tau Beta Sigma is a co-educational national honorary band sorority dedicated to serving college and band programs. The Sorority, headquartered at the historic Stillwater Station in Stillwater, OK, numbers 3,000 active members in 140 active chapters, and over 40,000 alumni.



Tau Beta Sigma operates primarily as a student service and leadership recognition society whose chief aim is to assist the Director of Bands in developing the leadership and enthusiasm that they require of their band. Our goals are not only to provide the band with organized and concentrated service activities, but to give our membership valid and wholesome experiences in organization, leadership, and social contacts.
