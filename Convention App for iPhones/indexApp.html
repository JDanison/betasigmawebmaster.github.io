2019 NCD Convention

Welcome to the 2019 NCD Convention! This app contains all kinds of information about this year's convention, including the weekend's schedule, maps, speaker biographies and much more!

The 2019 Convention of the North Central District of Kappa Kappa Psi and Tau Beta Sigma will be hosted at the Indianapolis Marriott East in Indianapolis, Indiana from March 29 to March 31 2019. Hosting this year's convention will be the Beta Sigma Chapter of Tau Beta Sigma and the Gamma Pi Chapter of Kappa Kappa Psi of Purdue University.

Registration will begin at 3 p.m. on Friday, March 29th in the Atrium. We look forward to seeing you in Indy!
